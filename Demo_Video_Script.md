# Demo Video Script

**Duration:** 2-3 minutes  
**Purpose:** Demonstrate the Stock Analysis Tool

---

## Video Outline

### 1. Introduction (30 seconds)

**What to show:**
- Title slide: "Stock Market Analysis 2016-2026"
- Brief introduction: "Hello, I'm Yao Jiamei. This is my ACC102 mini assignment - an interactive stock analysis tool."

**What to say:**
"Hi, I'm Yao Jiamei. Welcome to my stock market analysis project. In this video, I will show you my interactive data analysis tool that analyzes tech stocks from 2016 to 2026."

---

### 2. Problem and Audience (30 seconds)

**What to show:**
- Slide with project title and target audience

**What to say:**
"I chose to analyze four major technology companies: Apple, Microsoft, Google, and Amazon. My target audience is retail investors and business students who want to understand stock market trends. The goal is to help them see how tech stocks performed over the past 10 years."

---

### 3. Data Source (30 seconds)

**What to show:**
- Screenshot of Yahoo Finance website
- Code showing data download

**What to say:**
"I used Yahoo Finance as my data source. It's free and reliable. I downloaded historical stock prices using the yfinance library in Python. The time period is from January 2016 to April 2026."

---

### 4. Demo the Notebook (60 seconds)

**What to show:**
- Open Jupyter Notebook
- Run cells one by one:
  1. Import libraries
  2. Download data
  3. Show statistics
  4. Show visualizations

**What to say:**
"Let me show you the notebook. First, I import the libraries - pandas, numpy, matplotlib, and yfinance. Then I download the stock data. The code is quite simple - just a few lines to get all the data we need."

"Here you can see the basic statistics - mean price, standard deviation, minimum and maximum prices for each stock."

"Next, I created several visualizations. This chart shows stock prices over 10 years. You can see all four stocks have grown significantly."

"This chart shows Apple with moving averages. The 50-day and 200-day moving averages help identify trends."

"The last chart compares all stocks by normalizing their starting prices to 100. This makes it easy to compare performance."

---

### 5. Key Findings (30 seconds)

**What to show:**
- Summary table
- Key insights slide

**What to say:**
"From this analysis, I found that all tech stocks showed strong growth over 10 years. I also calculated volatility, which shows the risk level. The stock with highest returns also had higher volatility, which makes sense with the risk-return trade-off."

---

### 6. Conclusion (20 seconds)

**What to show:**
- Thank you slide with GitHub link

**What to say:**
"In conclusion, this tool helps investors understand long-term stock trends. It's simple but useful for educational purposes. Thank you for watching!"

---

## Tips for Recording

1. **Screen Recording:** Use OBS or built-in screen recorder
2. **Voice:** You can use text-to-speech (MiniMax is suggested in the assignment)
3. **Quality:** 720p or higher is fine
4. **Format:** MP4 recommended

## Upload Location

- YouTube (unlisted or public)
- Or any video platform with accessible link
